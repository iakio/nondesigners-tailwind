The Shakespeare Papers are snippets of amusing, tantalizing, peculiar, educative, unexpected, surprising, brilliant, intriguing, and occasionally controversial tidbits about the Shakespearean plays and sonnets.

Each beautifully designed, full-color, bimonthly, 16-page book- let focuses on one tiny aspect of these luminous works. Why, you ask?
